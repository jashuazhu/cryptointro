Introduction: Beyond the Headlines

The world of cryptocurrency is often painted in broad, chaotic strokes: overnight millionaires, devastating market crashes, and a barrage of impenetrable jargon. News cycles breathlessly track the price of Bitcoin, but the real story of this technology is far more complex and surprising than the daily charts suggest.
